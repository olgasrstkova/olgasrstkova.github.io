Films & videos

Metrodream

The author's reportage explores the anxiety attacks a female director experiences in the metro and confronts them with Peter, a train operator, and his own perspective on the profession and the environment in which he has to work every day. Their conversation gradually becomes a form of therapy and a space for sharing their inner fears, uncertainties, and worries.

12121

Psychological problems, alcoholism, and deliberate exclusion from society shaped the world of a young visual artist until a turning point marked by the numerals in the film's title. The moment of return to life is captured in an introspective portrait that seeks to convey the elusive fragility of balancing on the edge of non-being.
